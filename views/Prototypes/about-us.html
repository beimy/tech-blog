About Rabbit Hole

Learning is a journey, and we all get a little lost chasing those white rabbits of information and ideas. Here you will find a community of people who believe in putting the journey before the destination, who love to follow that thread of something that sparked their interest far longer than most others would, and enjoy sharing what they've learned along the way.
So come get lost in the sea of knowledge, experience other people journeys, and share a few of your own.
